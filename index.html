FAQ

Objections, answered

This is where most trainer sites lie. We don’t. You’ll get clarity on how it works.

How do I start?

Message on WhatsApp. Send age, height, weight, goal, and how many days/week you can train. If possible, add a photo.

Do I need a gym?

Gym is best. If you train at home, I’ll build around what you have. Results depend on execution and consistency.

Is this for beginners?

Yes. Beginners progress fast if they follow the plan. Advanced athletes need more precision. Both require discipline.

Will I get a strict diet?

No starvation, no fantasy meal plans. You get targets and structure. If you can’t follow it, it’s useless.

How fast will I see results?

Visible change can happen in weeks, but real transformation is months. Your speed depends on adherence, sleep, and weekly effort.

What does accountability look like?

Check-ins. Data. Adjustments. If you disappear, you lose momentum. The system works only when you show up.
